Amazon dominates the online marketplace around the world and it counts with more than 9.7 million of sellers worldwide where 1.9 million are selling actively today. Unfortunately, it’s a very competitive platform with their own constantly changing rules. My aim is to design a customized strategy for your business that ensures you have a constant stock of inventory on hand for continued growth and sales.

Out-of-stock products are lost sales. Logistika One covers all parts of inventory management for you so your products are always available, while minimizing inventory carrying costs.

Logistika One is for you if you are new to Amazon and you need an expert to help you to optimize your business or if you are a current seller who wants to delegate stock management and focus more on other core parts the business.
Flexible inventory plan
Stock planning must be flexible and easily adaptable to market changes. Seasonality of a product, peak season period, port or factory delays - there are many factors that affect stock planning and my mission is to detect any issue at an early stage and minimize the damage to your business.
It's more than just stock management
There is a big range of online stock management tools available on the market. However, these tools can be used only as a guide. My experience has shown that it is crucial to build trusting relationships with forwarders and factories. Constant communication prevents unnecessary delays and factories/forwarders are more eager to help you out when in need.
Amazon Policy
Amazon is infamous for its constant policy changes. As you may know, currently most of the sellers have stock limits – which means sellers cannot ship big batches of products directly to Amazon. My task is to make sure your IPI score goes up and your storage allowance grows.

About me

Hi! Welcome to Logistika One. My name is Lidia Faustova and I am a Logistics consultant.

I'm an experienced supply chain consultant, helping Amazon e-commerce businesses handle their supply chain. Having studied supply chain management, I'm versed in the theory, in addition to my hands on experience on the ground.

Originally from Moscow, I'm now based in Europe, in sunny Barcelona, Spain. I've operated with Amazon businesses, working with warehouses across the continent.

The supply chain for Amazon sellers is complex. More so than many other businesses. Rules constantly change and it can be time consuming to keep up with it all.

I have worked with Amazon businesses for over 4 years. Supply chain can be difficult, and I've had a few learnings along the way. I'm ready now to share this experience to help your business, providing you with all the best practices for Amazon optimisation that I've learned.

Here are a few things that I have been working on during the past few years:

- Expanded my client's businesses into new markets (Canada, UK and Europe).
- Managed international shipments from China, Germany, UK, South Korea, and the USA.
- Managed different types of products from very light small sized ones to oversized heavy products that could be managed only by FBM (fulfilment by merchant).
- Managed hazardous goods shipments (cosmetics, flamable items).
- Established a network of reliable forwarders and 3PLs.
- Set up and maintained good relationships with factories.
